Call of Duty: Black Ops 6 Zombies Bringing Back Perk That's Been Missing for a Decade

By Brett Byll — Published 3 days ago
7.3/10

Call of Duty: Black Ops 6

SUMMARY

  • Call of Duty: Black Ops 6 Zombies has hinted at the return of Double Tap, stirring excitement among players.
  • Activision hinted at Double Tap's return in Black Ops 6 Zombies through a community mailbag episode.
  • Other clues like a charm on the Repairman Operator and a skin on a shotgun suggest Double Tap's comeback.

The team for Call of Duty: Black Ops 6 Zombies appears to have strongly hinted at the return of Double Tap, leading to a wave of hype within the game's community. The latest season of Call of Duty: Black Ops 6 has received mixed reception, and the game's player count has dipped, but it appears the team behind the title is starting to bring back fan favorites in a push to drive engagement back up. Most recently, this seems to include a classic Zombies perk.

Double Tap was hinted at within a Call of Duty: Black Ops 6 Season 1 bundle. Players using the Repairman Operator noticed a charm on his belt that heavily resembled the logo used for the original Double Tap Perk. In addition to that clue, fans noticed that the Big Boomer skin on the Marine SP shotgun features the text "Double Tap" right above the weapon's foregrip. Now, there seem to be even more signs pointing towards the return of Double Tap.

6 Features That Need To Return To The Call Of Duty Franchise

Call of Duty has had its fair share of features throughout the years, but some definitely deserve to return in a future installment to the franchise.

In the March 26, 2025, episode of the COD POD, Activision's Stephanie Snowden seemingly confirmed that Double Tap is making its comeback in Call of Duty: Black Ops 6 Zombies. In the community mailbag segment of the episode, a fan asks for a hint about the next perk coming to the game. Snowden responds, "You sure can, partner!" while wearing a cowboy hat and using a Southern accent. Many have interpreted this as confirmation that Double Tap may be joining the roster of Black Ops 6 Zombies perks. Double tap was last seen in 2015's Black Ops 3.

Double Tap Seemingly Confirmed for Call of Duty: Black Ops 6 Zombies

Call of Duty players love Double Tap so much because of how useful it has consistently been in each title it's a part of. The perk boosts players' rate of fire, and in some games, upgraded versions of the perk have allowed players to shoot two bullets for the price of one. Because the utility of Double Tap is applicable in nearly every kind of situation a player can get themselves into, it's considered to be a part of the best perk loadouts in all of Call of Duty Zombies.

The cherry-picking tactic isn’t the only strategy DICE and EA have introduced to try and make the next iteration of the legendary franchise more successful than its predecessor. Recently, EA introduced Battlefield Labs, which allows gamers to test out the game and give feedback about their experiences. This collaborative approach between players and developers suggests that EA and DICE are doing everything they can to get it right this time around.

Despite the shortcomings of Battlefield 2042, the Battlefield series has produced many other great titles, such as Battlefield 1942 and Battlefield 2. Revisiting the franchise's history of classic games seems like a smart decision. For many fans, the hope is that EA and DICE will be able to capture the magic of those earlier titles and bring it into Battlefield 6.
